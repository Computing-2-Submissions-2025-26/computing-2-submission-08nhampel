MINESWEEPER

Clear the field. Trust nothing.

💣 10
⏱ 0

Goal

Reveal every cell on the grid that doesn't contain a mine. If you reveal a mine, you lose.

Rules

  • Numbers show how many of the 8 surrounding cells contain mines — use these to figure out where mines are hiding
  • Your first click is always safe — mines are placed after you reveal your first cell
  • Place flags on cells you think contain a mine to keep track

Controls

  • Left click a cell — Reveal a cell
  • Right click a cell — Place or remove a flag
  • Left click a number — If you've flagged all the mines around it, auto-reveals remaining neighbours

Keyboard

  • Tab — Focus the board
  • Arrow keys — Move between cells
  • Enter or Space — Reveal the focused cell
  • F — Flag or unflag the focused cell
  • Escape — Close instructions
